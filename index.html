About Us

20 Milestone

Prishtina REA, during its 24 years of operation, as Innovation, Digital Transformation and ICT Lead, has been one of the driving forces behind the development and growth of the Kosovar business advisory and consulting sector, innovation and ICT & digital sector, including Digital Agenda 2030, VET, e-ID Solution, non-formal professional education, EEN Country Coordinator, EIC Accelerator – Innovation Lead, Matching Grant Scheme Implementer (of 10.4 mil Euro), Digital Transformation and International Standard Certification in Tourism,  member of EBN and INSME, with a well networked and organized presence of donor-based programs and government modernization.
