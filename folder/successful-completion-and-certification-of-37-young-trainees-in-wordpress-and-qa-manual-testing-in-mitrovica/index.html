Successful Completion and Certification of 37 Young Trainees in WordPress and QA Manual Testing in Mitrovica

37 young individuals from Mitrovica have achieved excellent success by completing a 3-month training program for “WordPress and QA Manual Tester” and are now certified. This fantastic result reflects their dedication and skills in the field of information technology. Our young talents demonstrate their readiness to face the challenges of the IT market and are prepared to contribute to its development. 

 

The Youth Online and Upward (YOU) program is part of the KODE Project, implemented by the Ministry of Economy with the support of the World Bank. This initiative provides valuable opportunities for young individuals to develop their skills in the field of technology, preparing them for a successful career in the world of information technology.
